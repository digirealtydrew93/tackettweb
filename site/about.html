About Us

Our Story

Tackett Brothers Hauling was founded in 2015 by two brothers with a mission to provide fast, reliable, and professional hauling services to Columbus-area businesses. What started as a local operation has grown into the trusted choice for pallet and scrap removal throughout central Ohio.

We understand that businesses need dependable hauling partners who show up on time, work efficiently, and treat property with respect. That's the Tackett Brothers difference.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We show up when we say we will.
  • Professionalism: Courteous, knowledgeable team members.
  • Quality Service: No shortcuts, just honest work.
  • Community: Supporting local Columbus businesses.
  • Sustainability: Responsible recycling and disposal.
