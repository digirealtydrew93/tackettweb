How quickly can you pick up pallets?

We typically offer same-day or next-day pickup for most requests. Submit your request through our form or call for immediate availability.

Do you handle damaged or broken pallets?

Yes, absolutely. We remove all pallets regardless of condition - damaged, broken, or mixed pallets are no problem for us.

What areas do you service?

We serve Columbus and surrounding areas including Worthington, Westerville, New Albany, and Sunbury. Call or submit a request to confirm service in your specific area.

Is there a minimum or maximum order?

No minimums or maximums. We handle single pallets to large bulk removal orders. Just let us know what you need.

How are you priced?

Our pricing is competitive and transparent - no hidden fees. We'll provide a quote based on your specific needs.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured and licensed to operate in Ohio. Your property is protected.

Can you remove other types of items?

We specialize in pallets and metal scrap. We don't take broken skids or other items unless they're metal. Call us to confirm if your specific item qualifies.

What happens to the items you remove?

We practice responsible disposal and recycling. Pallets go to recyclers, scrap materials are processed appropriately, and usable items may be donated.
